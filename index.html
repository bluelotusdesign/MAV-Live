Welcome to Muscle Activation Vancouver

Are you dealing with acute or chronic injuries? Have you been seeing a physiotherapist, chiropractor, massage therapist, or other types of treatment for help, but are not satisfied with the results?

At Muscle Activation Vancouver (MAV), we understand pain and look at it differently than traditional treatment protocols.

We believe that pain is an indicator of a problem, but not necessarily the actual source of the problem.

We allow the body to tell us what is wrong, using a systematic approach to restore muscular balance:
Muscle Activation Techniques (MAT®)
